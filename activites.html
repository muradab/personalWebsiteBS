The Ethnological Museum
It's found at 6 kilo Addis Ababa universty main campus. The institutes building was built by Italin invasion in 1930's. later it become a royal palace after the emperor came from England.After a decades the emperor left the palace moved to 4 kilo. the emperor just not left the palace he changed it to a university kedamawi Haile Silase University and now it becomes Addis Ababa University
me and my friends dormitary were at 4 kilo. we went there for registration office for a withdrawal step. the entrance of the museum show some artcle or history about the place written in english. some of best ethiopian authors clothes and some of their utilities are found at the groud floor. at first floor thier is a tour that from birth to death in beautiful way. some of the emperors things like their gifts and bed are also in the museum. not only just this their are many ethiopian traditional musical instruments like 'kebero', 'washint' 'kirar'.
It was a fun day and we learned so many things from the museum. I know I didn't mention everything at the museum because I want to readers explore them.

The National Museum
It was a shiny bright day me and my dormmates get ready to go class. Unfortunately the lecturer was absent so we were bored and when we were going to our dorms we found other lads and decided to visit the museum.

As one can undertand from the name its one of the big museums.it has many collections of items ranging from rings to crowns to stones of ancient ages. it also have many fossil findings from lucy to ardi selam etc.we got chances to take a pic with the fossils.unlike the other places this museum has many visitors specially tourists.
